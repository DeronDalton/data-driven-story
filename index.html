MUSIC FESTIVALS & MULTICULTURAL MILLENNIALS

Forty percent of millennials identify as African-American, Asian-American or Hispanic, making millennials more racially and ethnically diverse than any previous generation in the U.S. Multicultural millennials are spending more on music annually, and are attending more live concerts and music festivals than non-Hispanic white consumers, making them an important group for companies to market.
