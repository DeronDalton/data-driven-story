MULTICULTURAL MILLENNIALS & THE MUSIC INDUSTRY

Multicultural music consumers are driving the music industry, spending more on live concerts, music festivals and accounting for 31 percent of total spending on music. This group is also influencing the music market by creating new ways to discover, buy and share music. Neilsen reports say companies that wish to understand the future of music need to pay close attention to this growing demographic's consumer habits.
